Zdenek “Z” Nemec is a computer systems researcher and architect. Author of the innovative API description language API Blueprint. He is also author of data modeling language–MSON. He currently works at Apiary as the Director for domain specific languages, product manager and research lead for API design.

He has a strong background in the distributed systems and client the problematic including user experience design. Zdenek loves interacting with teams, customers users and helping them to succeed.

Zdenek is interested in the structure of organization, system understanding, semantics and data modeling. He is advocate of Conway’s Law and therefore puts strong emphasis on the quality and clarity of communication as well as on the well-being of teams and individuals.
